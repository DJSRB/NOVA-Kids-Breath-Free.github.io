Breath Free - A NOVA-Talk

-Breath Free is a prevention design that raises awareness of the threatening consequences of resorting to e-cigarettes and being exposed to the chemicals of vape smoke. The talk will be open to all levels of high school students where an experienced adult will facilitate the discussion as he/she shares experiences and journeys in quitting.

-The talk will have a detailed description on everything that is negative about vaping, ranging from the health dangers to the potentially threatening effects from e-cigarette use.
